Design From Scratch

September 12, 2020

3:00 PM - 5:00 PM (+08)

Google meet

Design from Scratch with Edwin Capalla is the first instalment of the Fundamentals of Design series that aims to help attendees, particularly beginners, grasp the basic concepts of design such as line, shape, hierarchy, contrast, and composition. In addition, the speaker will also talk about color theory and provide resources for participants to implement what they have learned.
